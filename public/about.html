About MindWorth

MindWorth is a boutique AI consulting firm based in McKinney, Texas. Our mission is to demystify artificial intelligence and make it practical for small and medium‑sized businesses. With a combined background in business strategy, operations and machine learning, we bring the right blend of experience to help you succeed.

Our story & values

We started MindWorth because we saw too many small businesses struggle to navigate the fast‑evolving world of AI. As a three‑person team, we know what it’s like to wear many hats—and we’re passionate about helping you leverage technology to free up your time, improve quality and grow sustainably. We value transparency, ethical AI and long‑term partnership with our clients.

Meet our team

  • AI Strategist: Guides your transformation with tailored roadmaps and ROI models.
  • Operations & Client Success Lead: Ensures smooth delivery, clear communication and ongoing support.
  • Machine Learning Engineer: Designs, builds and tunes models to solve your unique business challenges.

We collaborate closely with you and your employees, empowering everyone to adopt AI with confidence.

Why choose us?

Unlike larger consultancies, we work with a limited number of clients at a time so we can provide personal attention and adapt to your evolving needs. Our vendor‑neutral approach means we recommend the right tools for your budget and goals. And because we’re entrepreneurs ourselves, we understand the constraints and opportunities faced by small businesses.
