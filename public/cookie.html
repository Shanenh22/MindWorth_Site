What is our cookie policy?

This page explains how and why we use cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to enable essential functionality and help us understand how our site is used. We use them to improve your browsing experience, measure analytics and personalize content.

How do we use cookies?

We use necessary cookies to remember your preferences and keep the site running. Analytics cookies help us understand usage patterns so we can enhance performance. We do not use cookies for advertising or share cookie data with third parties. Where required by law, we display a consent banner allowing you to accept or decline non‑essential cookies.

How can you manage cookies?

You can modify cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or via your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete or block cookies entirely. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.
