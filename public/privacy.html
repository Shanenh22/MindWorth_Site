What is MindWorth’s privacy policy?

We take data privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you interact with our website and services. We only collect data necessary to deliver the services you request and never sell personal information. When processing sensitive data, we adhere to the principles of data minimization, purpose limitation, security and transparency. For high‑risk AI activities we perform impact assessments, document our legal basis for processing and implement appropriate safeguards.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal details you provide via contact forms (e.g., name, email, phone) and usage data through analytics tools. We use this data only to respond to inquiries, improve services and analyze site performance. We use cookies solely for necessary functionality and analytics. You may opt out of non‑essential cookies at any time.

How do we protect your data?

Our systems employ encryption, access controls and secure hosting. We limit access to your data to authorized personnel and store information only as long as needed to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. We comply with applicable data‑protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, and honour requests to view, correct or delete your data.

How can you exercise your rights?

You can request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal data by emailing privacy@mindworth.com. If you have any concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us. We will respond within 30 days.
